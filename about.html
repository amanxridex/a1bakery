A1 Bakery

50+ Years

A Legacy of Baking Excellence

Founded in 1974, A1 Bakery began as a small corner shop with a big dream: to bring authentic French baking to Canadian homes.

Today, with over 150 locations from coast to coast, we remain committed to that original vision. Every loaf, every pastry, every cake is crafted using time-honored techniques passed down through generations.
